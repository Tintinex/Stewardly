How it works

Up and running in minutes, not months

Stewardly is purpose-built for HOA boards — no IT team required. If you can use email, you can use Stewardly.

1

Set up your community

Enter your building details, upload your unit roster, and invite your board members. Your workspace is ready in under 10 minutes.

2

Connect your finances

Securely link your association's bank accounts using our encrypted bank connection. Transactions sync automatically every day.

3

Invite your homeowners

Send residents a one-click invite to access their personal portal — dues, community updates, and the message board, all in one place.

4

Run your board, effortlessly

Hold your next meeting in Stewardly, assign action items on the spot, and let the platform do the follow-up for you.
